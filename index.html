WebService-EBC by rfilipo

Retrieve feeds, news and media from EBC

WebService-EBC Version 0.01 2011-December Simple spider to fetch news and media from EBC (Empresa Brasileira de Comunicacao) The EBC website serve a rss feed that is the main basis for this module. The spider finds some id tags and classes from the html codes in EBC to parse the news and media.
